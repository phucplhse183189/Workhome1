Grilled Chicken

Ingredients:

  • 1 whole chicken
  • Spices: salt, pepper, garlic
  • Fresh herbs for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the chicken and spices.
  2. Marinate the chicken for 30 minutes.
  3. Grill until golden brown.

Vegetable Stir-Fry

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups mixed vegetables
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

Instructions:

  1. Heat oil in a pan.
  2. Add vegetables and stir-fry for 5 minutes.
  3. Add soy sauce and cook for another 2 minutes.
